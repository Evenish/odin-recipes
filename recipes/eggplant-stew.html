Eggplant Stew
Description

This Greek-inspired eggplant stew is a delicious, hearty meal for the colder days. It is made with simple ingredients but don't be deceived by the simplicity; this meal is full of flavors that make it so comforting to eat on a winter night.

Ingredients
  • 1 kg eggplants, cut into cubes
  • 1 large yellow onion
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 carrot
  • 6 large garlic cloves
  • 2 dry bay leaves
  • 1 to 1.5 tsp sweet paprika
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp dry oregano
  • 3/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 800g can tomatoes
  • 2 450g can chickpeas
  • Fresh herbs
  • Salt
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
Steps
  1. Sweat eggplants by sprinkling diced eggplants with salt and set aside for 20 minutes. Preheat oven to 200 C.
  2. Heat 1/4 C olive oil in a large braiser until shimmering. Add onions, peppers, and carrot. Cook for 2-3 minutes, then add garlic, bay leaf, spices, and a dash of salt. Cook another minute, until fragrant.
  3. Add eggplant, tomatoes, chickpeas and stir to combine.
  4. Bring to boil for 10 minutes. Remove from stove top, cover and transfer to oven.
  5. Cook in oven for 45 minutes until egggplant is fully cooked to very tender.
  6. Remmove from oven, add a drizzle of olive oil, garnish with fresh herbs. Serve hot or at room temperature.
