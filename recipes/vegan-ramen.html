Vegan Ramen

Description

This Vegan Ramen is as much of an umami-bomb as non-vegan ones. It's topped with a fried crispy tofu, miso-glazed carrots and baby bokchoy. Garnishing with green onions not only provide a nice bright green touch, but gives crunch and aroma, while chili garlic sauce adds a beautiful, mild heat to wake everything up.

Ingredients

  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1 3-in piece ginger
  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • 6 C vegetable stoclk
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 15g dehydrated shiitake mushrooms
  • 1 Tbsp white miso paste
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 230 g ramen noodles

Toppings

  • 1/2 C chopped green onion
  • 300g extra-firm tofu, fried
  • Miso-glazed carrots
  • Miso-glazed baby bok choy

Steps

  1. Heat a large pot over medium-high heat. When oil is hot, add garlic, ginger and onioon. Saute for 5-8 minutes or until onion has developed a slight sear.
  2. Add 1C of vegetable broth to deglaze pan. Use a whisk or a wooden spoon to scrape up any bits that are stuck to the bottom of the pan to enhance flavor of the broth.
  3. Add remaining 5C of vegetable broth, soy sauce and dehydrated mushrooms.
  4. Bring to a simmer, reduce heat to low and simmer for at least 1 hour.
  5. Add miso paste at the end.
  6. Prepare toppings 30 minutes before serving.
  7. Divide broth between four bowls and add noodles and toppings.
